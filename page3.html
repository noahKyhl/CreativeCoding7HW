Contest Rules & Requirements

To participate in our tournaments, please review the following requirements:

  • All players must be at least 18 years old.
  • Players must register at least 24 hours before the tournament begins.
  • Sportsmanlike conduct is expected at all times.
  • Entry fees must be paid upon registration.

Available Games

  • 8-Ball
  • 9-Ball
  • 10-Ball
